During the internship, I participated in 2 Community Day events, each providing new knowledge, valuable skills, and opportunities to connect with the AWS Vietnam community.

### [Event 1](Event_1/index.md)
&emsp;**Event name:** AWS Community Day 2026

&emsp;**Date:** 05/23/2026

&emsp;**Location:** At the AWS office (details in the event report)

&emsp;**Role in the event:** Attendee

&emsp;**Brief description of content and main activities:** The event shared best practices for modern application design, provided practical knowledge on Amazon CloudFront, and introduced advanced AI tools to help optimize the development lifecycle. Speakers from leading companies such as VPBank, Cloud Kinetics, and GoTymeX shared insights on real-world projects from the 36H Code competition, the AI-first Mindset trend, the Track Line Content Mean thinking framework, LLM settings configuration, and performance optimization with Amazon CloudFront.

&emsp;**Results or value gained:** Learned skills for optimal role division within a team, leveraging the power of GenAI in workflows, applying the Goal → Situation → Constraints → Evidence framework to present technical solutions, and gained a solid understanding of LLM configuration and Amazon CloudFront. Had the opportunity to connect with experts from major enterprises and bridge the gap between theory and practice.

### [Event 2](Event_2/index.md)
&emsp;**Event name:** AWS AI & Cloud Operations Workshop

&emsp;**Date:** 06/12/2026

&emsp;**Location:** At the AWS office (details in the event report)

&emsp;**Role in the event:** Attendee

&emsp;**Brief description of content and main activities:** The workshop covered emerging trends in applying AI Agents to cloud infrastructure operations, DevOps, and system incident handling. Topics included AgenticOps for Your Cloud, AI Voice with low-latency Vietnamese language processing, technical aspects of DevOps Agents, real-world case studies, and AI applications in HR recruitment. Speakers from Noventiq, CloudThinker, Revve AI, Renova Cloud, and Cloud Kinetics shared their practical experience.

&emsp;**Results or value gained:** Gained a clear understanding of the role of AI Agents in cloud operations, learned techniques for building low-latency AI Voice applications, and understood how enterprises apply AI to recruitment and onboarding. Able to apply this knowledge to design operations for serverless systems, optimize costs with an observability-driven approach, and improve user experience in AWS projects.
